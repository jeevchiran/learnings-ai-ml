Pandas EDA & Visualization

9 modules covering pandas-based exploratory data analysis: cleaning, missing values, outliers, datetime feature engineering, and univariate/bivariate/multivariate visualization. One shared 30-row NYC taxi-trip sample threads through every module.

  • Module 1: EDA Foundations & First Look
    shape, dtypes, head/info/describe — what you must know before cleaning anything.
  • Module 2: Missing Values
    isna ratios, dropna vs fillna, and why mean isn't always the right fill.
  • Module 3: Data Quality & Invalid Values
    negative fares, zero-distance trips, and boolean-mask business rules.
  • Module 4: Outlier Detection & Treatment (IQR)
    Tukey fences, cap vs remove, and telling a real error from a flat-rate fare.
  • Module 5: Datetime Parsing & Feature Engineering
    turning timestamp strings into hour, weekday, and duration features.
  • Module 6: Univariate Visualization
    histograms, boxplots, and reading skew before comparing variables.
  • Module 7: Bivariate Analysis
    scatter plots, groupby, and why a correlation can hide in dirty data.
  • Module 8: Multivariate Analysis
    correlation matrices and pivot tables across several variables at once.
  • Module 9: Capstone — End-to-End EDA Workflow
    chaining every prior step into one repeatable pipeline.
